About Us

We are called EOW, which stands for Education of Will, corresponding to the will of our team mainly to overcome the difficulties in obtaining quality education. At the same time, it can also be understood as End of week and End of watch. The former represents our relaxed and happy atmosphere, and the latter represents our cautious attitude.

Lamplighter Charity Program

In Rwanda, Africa, about 40% of the population of nearly 13 million are students and children under the age of 14, and 66% of them are still without lights at night. The shortage of photovoltaic resources has made the conditions for children's education to suffer a major challenge. The lack of economic resources makes them unable to purchase photovoltaic educational facilities.
